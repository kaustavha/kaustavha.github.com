Hello

I'm an engineer in training at the University of Waterloo, Canada's most innovative university.Or that's what they tell us. Engineering is hard. 30 hours a week of classes followed by homework, so it doesn't really leave much time. I built this website as a weekend project, learning HTML and jQuery, during this term. And I'll talk about my life so far for some background. I spent most of my childhood in Kolkata, India, with a brief stint in Canada, moving from Brampton to Calgary, then back to India. For a variety of reasons, I ended up switching schools many times over the course of my life. I've spent time in two different Indian education systems, and the Canadian school system, finally graduating from The Cambridge School in Kolkata, with a British O/A level diploma. I've travelled with my familiy to many parts of India. Seen the world from the side of the rich, with their house parties in high rise apartments, and the rich at heart, playing soccer on the dusty streets of my hometown. I've been a member of a rowing club, participating in races. I've trained hard, running 5k and 10k runs five days a week for the sake of fitness. A life threatening bout of typhoid when I was around 14 left me unable to compete properly. Since then, I've played soccer at a club, and for my school, helping my team win gold in an inter-school tournament. And tried to stay in relatively decent shape. I've had a keen interest in technology since I was a child. Assembled a computer and set it up with Linux when I was 12. More recently, I rooted my phone, and flashed CyanogenMod 10 on it. And I'm working on setting up a raspberry pi. I've helped a friend in India set up a web development company, helping him with the web development and Search Engine Optimization for his websites. I'm not sure where he ended up, but last time I checked, he was working for a bigger web development firm back home. I have no relevant work experience, but I make up for it with the ability to learn quickly and apply my knowledge effectively.
