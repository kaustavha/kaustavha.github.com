I'll tell you a little about my life if you're interested.
I spent most of my childhood in Kolkata, India, with a brief stint in Canada, moving from Brampton to Calgary, then back to India. For a variety of reasons, I ended up switching schools many times over the course of my life.

I've spent time in two different Indian education systems, and the Canadian school system, finally graduating from The Cambridge School in Kolkata, with a British O/A level diploma. I've travelled with my familiy to many parts of India. Seen the world from the side of the rich, with their house parties in high rise apartments, and the rich at heart, playing soccer on the dusty streets of my hometown.

I've been a member of a rowing club, participating in races, and training hard with 5k or 10k runs. A life threatening bout of typhoid when I was around 14 left me unable to compete properly. Since then, I've played soccer at a club, and for my school, helping my team win gold in an inter-school tournament. And tried to stay in relatively decent shape.

I've had a keen interest in technology since I was a child. Assembled a computer and set it up with Linux when I was 13. More recently, I rooted my phone, and flashed CyanogenMod 10 on it. I've set up a raspberry pi, and I'm working on writing code for a LeapMotion controller as time permits.

I'm a resident of Velocity, a start-up incubator / residence on the University campus, with some incredibly smart people. Learning from them, about everything from evolution, to technology to life in general. It's a great place, and I feel my intellect has definitely benifited from the 4 short months I've lived here so far.

I've helped a friend in India set up a web development company, helping him with the web development and Search Engine Optimization for his websites. I'm not sure where he ended up, but last time I checked, he was working for a bigger web development firm back home. I have very little work experience, but I make up for it with the ability to learn quickly and apply my knowledge effectively.
