Templater

Templater is a tool letting you use variables and write a template and use it over and over again. Save time! More time for beer!

Just write 'var-' followed by the name you want for the variable.

Here's an example, its the var-adjective example ever! But you should use this thing so you have to write less for var-thing. var-x + var-y = var-z, but also more free time for you! You can also use it for cover letters, invitations, sales e-mails and so forth.

Hit the > in the toolbar to see the magic! And just delete all this text to get started writing your own email/cover-letter/applications.

You can use bold, italics, both and urls just by highlighting the text and selecting them from the tiny options box that appears above it.

Quotes are easy to add too!

For questions and open source info, Click that little question mark at the bottom left of the screen.
